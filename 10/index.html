Statement 10 of 30

10

Our job is not to tame the machine, but to awaken the human next to it.

Why this matters

GenAI will surge forward, indifferent to our rules. Our work is to nurture the inner compass of empathy, discernment, and ethical vision which keeps education anchored in humanity. The true horizon of learning is not control; it is conscience.

A question for reflection

What human capacities do you most want your teaching to strengthen in an age of capable machines?
