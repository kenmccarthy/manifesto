Statement 13 of 30

13

Institutions must lead ethically, not just efficiently.

Why this matters

Universities cannot hand their conscience to vendors. Universities must anchor decisions in the core values of equity, privacy, and sustainability rather than simply cost. Institutional courage is what makes staff integrity possible.

A question for reflection

Where do cost and convenience currently outweigh values in your institution's technology decisions?
