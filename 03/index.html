Statement 03 of 30

03

GenAI does not replace thinking - it reveals the cost of not thinking.

Why this matters

GenAI can produce fluent nonsense and hidden bias. When we outsource thought, we see how easily we mistake coherence for truth. The greatest lesson is humility: GenAI shows how much effort authentic thinking still requires.

A question for reflection

When has GenAI's fluency almost convinced you of something that turned out to be wrong - and what did that teach you?
